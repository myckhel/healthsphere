### 1. Step-by-Step Flow of User Interaction
The user journey for HealthSphere AI transforms a traditional, paper-bound health-centre visit into a streamlined, digital experience. Here is the step-by-step flow based on the patient journey:
*   **Step 1: Ingestion & Digitization (Check-In):** When a patient arrives with an old paper medical card, reception scans it. An AI Vision Data Agent uses Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to instantly extract the handwritten text and map it to a clean, structured digital Electronic Health Record (EHR) database.
*   **Step 2: Intelligent Triage & Routing:** The patient interacts with a self-service tablet kiosk or a familiar platform like a WhatsApp chatbot. Powered by an AI Triage Agent utilizing indigenous language models, it screens the patient's symptoms and automatically routes them to the correct specialist or care queue.
*   **Step 3: Ambient Clinical Consultation:** During the actual consultation, an Ambient AI Scribe actively listens to the interaction. It extracts key medical intelligence and auto-generates specialty-specific clinical notes directly into the EMR, allowing the physician to focus entirely on the patient without typing.
*   **Step 4: Automated Follow-Up & Outreach:** A few days post-visit, an AI Outreach Agent automatically sends a context-aware SMS or WhatsApp message in the local language to monitor the patient's recovery. If the patient reports lingering or worsening symptoms, the AI instantly flags their profile, prompting a human nurse to intervene and book a follow-up.
